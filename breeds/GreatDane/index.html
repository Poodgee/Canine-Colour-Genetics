Quick Guide

For Experts: Select the alleles for both parents. The engine calculates the Punnett square, including the M-Locus (Merle) and H-Locus (Harlequin) interactions.

For Beginners: Use the ? buttons to see what each gene does. Choose a combination for the Sire and Dam, then click Predict!

Pro Tip: Remember that Harlequin patterns only appear if the dog also carries the Merle gene!
